Privacy Policy for Brainrot Blast

Last Updated: January 2, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Brainrot Blast ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile game application ("the App").

Information We Collect

1. Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Game progress, gameplay statistics, session duration, features used
  • Crash Reports: Technical information about app crashes and errors via Firebase Crashlytics

2. Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our App. This includes:

  • Game level completions
  • Time spent in-app
  • Feature usage patterns
  • General app performance metrics

This data is anonymized and does not personally identify you.

3. Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data
  • General location data (country/region level)

For more information about Google's data practices, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  1. App Functionality: To provide and maintain the game experience
  2. Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve the App
  3. Technical Support: To diagnose and fix technical issues
  4. Advertising: To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  5. Analytics: To understand user behavior and optimize game features

Data Storage and Local Storage

The App uses local storage (Hive and SharedPreferences) to save:

  • Game progress and achievements
  • User preferences and settings
  • High scores and statistics

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Display advertisements
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Data Collected: Advertising ID, IP address, ad interactions

Firebase Analytics

  • Purpose: App analytics and usage tracking
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Data Collected: Anonymous usage statistics, device information

Firebase Crashlytics

  • Purpose: Crash reporting and debugging
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Data Collected: Crash logs, device information, app state at crash time

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The App is designed for general audiences and does not target children specifically.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at the email address below, and we will take steps to remove that information.

Your Rights and Choices

Advertising Choices

  • iOS: You can limit ad tracking by enabling "Limit Ad Tracking" in your device settings (Settings > Privacy > Advertising)
  • Android: You can reset your advertising ID or opt-out of personalized ads in Google Settings

Data Deletion

Since most data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data by:

  • iOS: Uninstalling the app
  • Android: Clearing app data from Settings or uninstalling the app

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect information collected by the App. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
